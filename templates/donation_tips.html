Blood Donation Tips

Before Donation

  • Get plenty of sleep the night before
  • Eat a healthy meal within 2-3 hours before donating
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Bring valid ID and list of medications

During Donation

  • Wear comfortable clothing
  • Relax and stay calm
  • Inform staff if you feel unwell

After Donation

  • Drink extra fluids for the next 24-48 hours
  • Avoid strenuous physical activity for the rest of the day
  • Don't smoke for at least 2 hours
  • Keep the bandage on for at least 4-5 hours

Quick Facts

Donation Time

Takes only 8-10 minutes

Recovery Time

Body replenishes within 24 hours

Next Donation

Wait 56 days between donations
