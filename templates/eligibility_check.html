Can I Give Blood?

Basic Eligibility Requirements

You can donate if you:
  • Are at least 17 years old (16 with parental consent in some states)
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds
  • Are in good general health
  • Haven't donated whole blood in the last 56 days
You cannot donate if you:
  • Have a cold, flu, or other acute illness
  • Have certain chronic illnesses
  • Are pregnant
  • Have traveled to certain countries in the past year

Health Conditions to Consider

Condition Can Donate? Notes
Diabetes Yes If well controlled
High Blood Pressure Yes If controlled with medication
Recent Surgery Maybe Wait 6-12 months
Cancer No Active or recent treatment
