Frequently Asked Questions

Generally, you can donate blood if you:
  • Are at least 16-17 years old (age requirements vary by location)
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds
  • Are in good health
  • Haven't donated blood in the last 56 days

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). Platelet donation can be done every 7 days, up to 24 times a year.

The entire process takes about an hour, but the actual blood donation only takes about 8-10 minutes.
